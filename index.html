Stock-Bond Portfolio Projections

You have $1,000 and you’d like to invest in the stock market but you’d like to balance the risk and rewards of stocks and bonds in your portfolio. This Tableau visualization will allow you to evaluate the potential return on investment with a variable ratio of stock to bonds. The visualization shows the growth of your $1,000 in the 2008 recession or over the past two years which is characterized as a growth period.

Urban Sound Classification

“Tak, tak, tak.” It sounds like they’re putting up a new high rise on your block. Urban soundscapes are hectic places where the sounds of people and machinery are always a commotion. In order to make sense of the busy noises of a city, we developed a data science algorithm to classify these sounds. The classifier is able to distinguish between 10 different urban sounds including a jackhammer, siren, and children playing with 90% accuracy.
